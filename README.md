# EVA Cognitive Assistant PoC Tool

** National and Kapodistrian University of Athens **
** Faculty of Economics and Political Sciences **
** Department of Business Administration **

This repository contains an early-stage Proof of Concept (PoC) tool developed as part of my Bachelor Thesis on Reliable Artificial Intelligence-Driven Cognitive
Assistants for Extravehicular Activity Missions: A Theoretical Framework and Implementation Proposal for Astronaut Safety .

The tool demonstrates how an astronaut support interface could visualize basic EVA-related information, such as suit status, biometric values, communication status, and environmental risk alerts. It also includes a simple AI assistant named Alice, which can provide voice-based warnings when a possible danger, such as collision risk, is detected.

The purpose of this PoC is not to provide a complete or operational EVA-ready system. The current version uses simulated values, visual logic, and basic browser-based functionality. It is intended only to show how theoretical concepts such as situational awareness, AI assistance, warning visualization, voice interaction, and human-centered decision support could be represented in a simple prototype.

Future development could include real sensor integration, stronger AI functionality, cybersecurity mechanisms, improved validation, and more realistic simulation testing.
